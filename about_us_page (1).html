About PassionView Solutions Limited

Empowering Africa through tech innovation and skills development

Who We Are

PassionView Solutions Limited is a tech innovation and capacity development company focused on equipping Nigerian youth with essential digital skills. Through cutting-edge training, we aim to bridge the digital divide and unlock global tech opportunities for local talent.

See PassionView in Action

Our Mission

To deliver practical, hands-on technology education that prepares young Africans for sustainable careers in tech.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading provider of digital transformation and ICT empowerment programs.

Our Journey

2020 - Founded

PassionView Solutions Limited was established with a mission to empower African youth through tech.

2021 - Launched First Web Development Bootcamp

Trained 100+ students in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript across Nigeria.

2022 - Expanded to Data Analytics & Cybersecurity

Introduced hands-on programs in Power BI, SQL, and Ethical Hacking.

2024 - Partnered with NYSC and ITF

Recognized as a key SAED ICT training partner across northern Nigeria.

Certifications & Partners

We offer certification upon completion of all tech training programs.
